A Quiet Escape by the Vembanad Lake

Step into a world where time slows down and nature takes the lead. Nestled along the tranquil backwaters of Kumarakom, our resort offers breathtaking views of Vembanad Lake, gentle breezes through swaying palms, and an atmosphere designed for complete relaxation. Whether you’re enjoying a quiet morning by the water, savoring traditional Kerala cuisine, or unwinding in the comfort of your room, every moment here invites you to reconnect with yourself and the beauty around you.



Our resort pool offers a clean, spacious setting designed for comfort and relaxation. The clear, well-maintained water is temperature-controlled, making it perfect for a refreshing swim at any time of day.



Our food service is designed to be convenient, fresh, and satisfying throughout your stay. With attentive staff, timely service, and a clean, welcoming dining space, we aim to make every meal simple, enjoyable, and part of a pleasant overall experience at the resort.



Our resort is designed to make families feel comfortable, relaxed, and well taken care of throughout their stay. A dedicated kids’ play area provides a safe and engaging space where children can have fun and pet friendly policy so you can bring your whole family including pets!



Our luxurious conference hall offers a refined and professional setting for meetings, corporate events, and special gatherings. With dedicated event support, catering services, and attention to detail, we strive to make every business meeting or formal occasion smooth, efficient, and memorable.
